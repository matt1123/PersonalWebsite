Summary



I am a Junior at UNC Chapel Hill double majoring in Computer Science and Information Science.

Since I was born, I have been playing with computers, getting an feel for how my input would change what appeared on the screen and everything that happened in the back was magic (I am talking about the conditional styled charms that I would read about in Harry Potter). It wasn't until my first semester in college that I had touched any real code, finding out two things: first, some of my classmates had a much deeper understanding of programming, and second, I had a skill for learning how to program and learning many different languages.
